About Me

Story

Hi! I'm Justine Upano. I am passionate about design, analysis, and development of web application. I am more into Front End Development and my passion is all about creating practical and user-friendly UI. I also do Back End Development using PHP and MySQL.

I am a graduating student of Computer Engineering Technology in Technologial University of the Philippines - Cavite. I passion for programming started when I was introduced to Java in my 1st year of college. I like problem solving and the excitement as well as the relief of completing a programming exercise. I was hooked into Web development when I was in 3rd year of college. From there, I started self-studying about the technologies and coding patterns of popular developers in YouTube.

I started my experience by doing front end development from people I met in Discord and Facebook. They gave me the theme, goal, demographics , and preffered aesthetics of clients they met. From this, I continue to layout the design in Figma as well as wireframes. After the designs are approved, I proceed to development. Now, I try to apply for companies for more opportunities to further increase my knowledge about web development.
